Reporting Bugs & Glitches

HOW TO REPORT A BUG

To report a bug/glitch, you can either direct message us on Discord (Sonthoi2101#1987) - (Tom140112#7751) or join our Discord server and go to the forum bug-reporting/beta-bug-reporting. If you want to report a bug/glitch or multiple bugs/glitches, you can provide certain informations about it, such as:

  • How to replicate the bug
  • A detailed description of the bug
  • Screenshots of the bug being shown (optional)
  • What can it affect (gameplay, savecode, etc.) (optional)
